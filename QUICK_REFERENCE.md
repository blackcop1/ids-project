correctness | General overview |\n| **Precision** | False Positive Rate | When false alarms are costly |\n| **Recall** | False Negative Rate | When missing attacks is critical |\n| **F1-Score** | Balance of both | Default choice |\n\n### Model Selection Guide\n\n| Model | Speed | Accuracy | Interpretability | Use Case |\n|-------|-------|----------|-----------------|----------|\n| **Random
